    Our Mission

    "Sports do not build character. They reveal it." It is often said that the key to a sharp mind is a healthy body and therefore to make its students "healthy, wealthy and wise" IIT Ropar lays a huge emphasis on promoting sports activities among its students. And thus the sports become an integral part of the education process of the students. The students have various sports facilities which they enjoy every day on campus. Presently, IIT Ropar has one cricket field, three cricket practice pitches, four practice nets, three clay tennis courts, two volleyball courts, a football field, a hockey field, a gymnasium, three table tennis tables, a basketball court and a ground for different athletic events.

    Aarohan

    Aarohan, the annual sports fest of IIT Ropar, is an exhilarating celebration of athleticism, team spirit, and competition. Held with great enthusiasm and fervor, Aarohan brings together students from across the country to showcase their sporting prowess in a wide range of events. From track and field to indoor and outdoor sports, the festival offers a diverse array of competitions that cater to every sporting interest. Participants battle it out in an electrifying atmosphere, supported by cheering crowds and the camaraderie that comes with being part of a prestigious institute like IIT Ropar. Aarohan serves as a platform for athletes to test their skills, forge new friendships, and create lasting memories. With its vibrant energy and unwavering passion for sports, Aarohan has become an unmissable event on the calendar of sports enthusiasts in the region. We had a total of over 700 participants, with over 150 girls and 600 boys, who competed fiercely in 15 sporting events. It was a pleasure to see the participants from 15 different colleges, coming from as far as 2000 km to participate in the fest. More than 80 matches were played during the fest. We had over 50 officials, including referees, judges, and medical staff, who worked tirelessly to ensure the smooth running of the events. IIT Ropar were the overall General Champion of the AAROHAN’ 24 with 132 points.

    Inter IIT

    The Inter IIT Sports Meet, a grand annual extravaganza, unites the finest athletes from esteemed Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) in a display of unparalleled sporting prowess. With a resplendent blend of healthy competition, unwavering sportsmanship, and an extraordinary showcase of talent across diverse disciplines, it is a testament to the indomitable spirit that drives the participants to greatness. In the 56th edition, held with great fervor in Gandhinagar and Bombay, IIT Ropar proudly emerged as a beacon of excellence. Securing a remarkable 9th position in the overall championship, their achievements shone brightly with an impressive conquest in the esteemed female tennis championship. Additionally, the illustrious IIT Ropar athletes soared to new heights, clinching the silver & bronze medal in the weightlifting event in U69 & U62 categories respectively, while demonstrating their mettle with a remarkable 4th position in Women's Table Tennis and Men’s 800m in Athletics. These extraordinary achievements exemplify the unwavering dedication, perseverance, and relentless pursuit of greatness displayed by the phenomenal athletes from IIT Ropar, making them an epitome of sporting prowess and a source of immense pride.
