Achievements

The Chess Club of IIT Ropar was placed on 6th in INTER IIT Sports Fest.They were joint third.

  • In the first round our team won against IIT Mandi by 3.5-0.5
  • In the third round our team won against IIT Tirupati by 2.5-1.5
  • In the fourth round our team drew against IIT Hyderabad.
  • In the fifth round our team drew against IIT Gandhi Nagar.
  • In the sixth round our team drew against IIT Dharwad.
  • In the seventh round our team won against IIT Kanpur by 3-1.

The Team A of IIT Ropar were winners in Arohan Sports Fest and the Team B of IIT Ropar were the runner ups.

The chess club of IIT Ropar also participated in Chessler’s Chess Tournament which was conducted online.

In JUIT Sports Event our boy’s team was placed on 3rd position and our girl’s team was placed on 2nd postion.
